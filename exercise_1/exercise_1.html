Fruit Chutney




It's that time of the year again! So ready to harvest this season's pears and figs from my backyard. Why not a Pear & Fig chutney? Easily done!

  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4-5 Servings

    LIST OF INGREDIENTS

  • Pears, peeled, ends removed, quartered and seeded
  • Figs, stems removed, and cut in half
  • Raisins, golden seedless
  • Honey
  • apple cider vinegar
  • balsamic vinegar
  • cloves
  • allspice
  • vanilla extract
  • freshly ground black pepper
INGREDIENTS            QUANTITY
Pears      6
Figs      8
Raisins      1/4 cup
apple cider vinegar      1/4 Cup
apple cider vinegar      1/2 tsp
balsamic vinegar      1/2 tsp
cloves      1/2 tsp
allspice      1/2 tsp
vanilla extract      1/2 tsp
freshly ground black pepper      1 tsp

METHOD:

It's that time of the year again! So ready to harvest this season's pears and figs from my backyard. Why not a Pear & Fig chutney? Easily done!

  • In a medium saucepan, place the pears, figs, vinegars and heat over medium for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add all the spices and the honey and bring to a boil, stirring frequently to combine and prevent caramelization
  • Reduce to a simmer and stir occasionally to reduce the liquids, about 20 minutes
  • Add the black pepper and stir for another 15 minutes
  • When the mixture has thickened to a jammy consistency, you can spoon the chutney into 2 pint jars, filling one full and the second half way
  • Refrigerate for two or three days
  • Try this on nice slices of ham baked and cut off the bone, most pork cuts and roast lamb, or well-seasoned fowl.s
